# 🛡️ Vikings Vengeance

Welcome commander, today we unleash the fury of the Vikings in this epic vengeance event! 🛡️⚔️

## Troop Exchange Strategy

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Exchange your troops with a partner. Send all your troops out for most points. Make sure you coordinate so they send you troops back.

- Find a reliable partner for troop exchange
- Send your entire army to maximize points
- Coordinate timing to ensure mutual benefit
- Trust is key - choose your partner wisely

## Defense Preparation

> [!TIP]
> Keep your three strongest heroes at home to defend your city.

- Your top 3 heroes should remain stationed
- They provide crucial defense against attacks
- Don't send them out during exchanges

> [!WARNING]
> Be online throughout the event to monitor and respond quickly.

## HQ Targeting Rounds

> [!CAUTION]
> Rounds 10 and 20 target the HQ. Recall your closest line and send Amadeus, Chenko, or Yeonwoo as lead hero, with a maximum of 70,000 of your strongest Cavalry and Infantry. Do not send any archers!

### Round 10 & 20 Strategy:
- **Lead Heroes**: Amadeus, Chenko, or Yeonwoo only
- **Troop Composition**: Maximum 70,000 Cavalry + Infantry
- **No Archers**: They reduce effectiveness for HQ attacks
- **Timing**: Recall closest march and redirect immediately

## Defense Failure Protocol

> [!NOTE]
> If your city defenses fail twice, focus on reinforcing others instead of rebuilding your own attacks.

- After 2 defense failures, switch to support role
- Help alliance members maintain their positions
- Reinforce key players to keep alliance strong

## Healing Guidelines

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Do not heal until the full event is done or until instructed by R4/R5.

- Conserve healing resources for the final push
- Only heal when authorized by R4/R5 leaders
- Patience is key - save for when it matters most

## Victory Mindset

> [!TIP]
> Stay coordinated, stay online, and trust your alliance. Vikings Vengeance rewards the prepared and the persistent!

> Good luck in Vikings Vengeance, mighty commander! May your axes swing true and your defenses hold strong! 🛡️⚔️
