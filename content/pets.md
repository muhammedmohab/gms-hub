## 🧭 Why Pets Matter  
Pets give you:  
- ⚡ Utility (faster building, gathering, stamina)  
- 💥 Combat power (attack, lethality, squad size)  

> [!TIP]  
> A well-fed pet = a stronger kingdom 🐾

## 🧪 Pet Refinement (Grandma’s Secret Sauce 🧠)  

This is where pets become MONSTERS 💥  

- Uses **Taming Marks**  
- Buffs:  
  - ❤️ Health  
  - 💥 Lethality (VERY important!)  

> [!TIP]  
> These stats affect **ALL troop types** 🪖  

---

### 🎲 How Refinement Works  
It’s a little gamble 🎰  

- Roll stats → see result  
- If power goes UP ✅ → **Replace**  
- If power goes DOWN ❌ → **Retry**  

### 🤖 AUTO Button  
- Keeps good stats 👍  
- Discards bad ones 👎  

> Easy baking mode 🍪  

---

## 🌈 Refinement Rarity  

- ⚪ Grey  
- 🟢 Green

- 🟡 Gold  

> [!TIP]

## 🧾 Taming Marks Guide  

- ⚪ **Common Taming Marks** → Low rarity refining  
- 🔵 **Advanced Taming Marks** → Blue+ rarity  

---

## ⚠️ Grandma’s Warning  
- Don’t rush **Gold refinement** 🟡  
- It’s VERY expensive and hard  

> Focus on building multiple strong pets first 💛
