# 🌾 Farm Account Guide

Welcome commander! Today we unlock the power of farm accounts to supercharge your main account! 🌾💪

## What is a Farm Account?

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A farm account is a secondary account that you create and control to benefit your main account during events and daily activities. It's like having a trusted ally right in your pocket!

A farm account is essentially a second account in the same kingdom/alliance that you manage to generate additional resources, raid opportunities, and strategic advantages for your main account.

---

## Creating Your Farm Account

### Step 1: Download the Game on Another Device
- Use a second phone, tablet, or emulator
- Or create a new profile/user on your existing device
- The game supports multiple accounts on the same device through different profiles

### Step 2: Create a New Account
1. Launch the game on your new device/profile
2. Complete the tutorial (takes 5-10 minutes)
3. Choose your kingdom carefully - **must be the same kingdom as your main account**
4. Pick a name for your farm account (e.g., "MainName-Farm")

### Step 3: Join Your Alliance
> [!TIP]
> Make sure your farm account joins the same alliance as your main account so you can coordinate sharing and raids.

1. Find your alliance in the search menu
2. Send a join request
3. Have your R4/R5 approve it quickly

---

## Initial Farm Account Setup

### Rush to Unlock Key Features

> [!CAUTION]
> Don't get sidetracked with random building or troop training. Focus on unlocking the features you need for your main account.

**Priority Actions:**
1. Complete tutorials and initial quests
2. Build to level 10+ quickly to access events
3. Focus on Island development (get basic buildings)
4. Unlock the Tavern for troop recruitment
5. Prioritize any buildings needed for current events

### Resource Prioritization

> [!NOTE]
> Farm accounts don't need to be strong - they just need to generate resources and opportunities for your main account.

- **Gems**: Very limited - use only for critical event unlocks
- **Construction**: Focus only on necessary buildings
- **Troops**: Train basic troops for raiding (cheap units)
- **Resources**: Let them build naturally; don't rush

## Using Your Farm Account: Merchant Empire Strategy

This is where farm accounts shine! 🌟

### The Power of Dual Raiding

> [!IMPORTANT]
> With a farm account, you can raid the same high-value caravan twice (once per account), maximizing your loot from exceptional finds.

**How It Works:**
1. Your **farm account** finds or refreshes to a high-value caravan
2. **Share** that caravan location to your alliance in the event tab
3. Your **main account** raids it immediately
4. Your **farm account** raids the same caravan again

### Strategic Caravan Hunting

**Option 1: Hunt with Main Account First**
- Your main account scouts and finds a valuable caravan
- Share it to your farm account
- Farm account raids it before your main account does

**Option 2: Hunt with Farm Account First**
- Your farm account actively searches for mythics or high-value drops
- When found, immediately share to your main account
- Main account raids it, then farm account raids again

### Refresh Strategy with Farm Account

> [!TIP]
> Use your farm account to burn through refresh attempts to find guaranteed mythics, then share the best ones.

- Farm account refreshes caravans up to 6 times to guarantee a mythic
- If the guaranteed mythic has good loot, share it
- Main account raids it for guaranteed high-value loot
- Farm account gets its daily raids as bonus

## Farm Account Upgrade Path

### Beginner Phase (Days 1-7)
- **Goal**: Unlock core event features
- **Focus**: 
  - Island level 15-20
  - Basic troop production
  - Event access

### Early Farming Phase (Week 2-3)
- **Goal**: Consistent event participation
- **Investments**:
  - Tavern upgrades (for troop recruitment)
  - Warehouse for resource storage
  - Docks for faster troop deployment

### Mid Farming Phase (Month 2)
- **Goal**: Viable event competitor
- **Focus**:
  - Island level 25-35
  - Consistent resource generation
  - Stable troop force
  - Event score contribution

> [!NOTE]
> Don't rush to high levels - a level 15-25 farm account is often better than a level 40+ account because it's easier to manage and uses fewer resources.

### Optimal Farm Account Level
> [!TIP]
> Many experienced players keep farm accounts intentionally at levels 20-30. This is the "sweet spot" where you can:
> - Participate in most events
> - Not attract unnecessary attention from aggressive players
> - Maintain easy management alongside your main
> - Still generate valuable event opportunities

## Advanced Farm Account Tactics

### The Guaranteed Mythic Share
> [!IMPORTANT]
> This is the most powerful farm account tactic for Merchant Empire.

1. Use farm account gems (if needed) to refresh caravans
2. Get the guaranteed mythic after 6 refreshes
3. Check the loot - if exceptional (Artisan Visions, rare materials):
   - **Share immediately** to your main account
   - Main account raids for guaranteed mythic-level loot
4. Farm account gets second raid as well
5. Result: Main account gets TWO high-quality raids from one caravan

### Controlled Speed Advantage
- Farm account completes caravan routes faster (simpler build focus)
- Reports completion times to main
- Main can time raids accordingly for better timing

### Assist Farming
> [!NOTE]
> Each account gets one daily assist use. Use both for critical high-tier caravans.

- Farm account assists mythic caravans on main account
- Main account assists high-value caravans for farm account
- Double the assist bonuses on your best finds

## Important Rules & Etiquette

### Account Linking & Security
> [!CAUTION]
> Link both accounts to the same email or backup system. If you lose access, you lose your farming advantage!

- Use a secure password manager
- Link accounts to same Google/Apple ID if possible
- Enable two-factor authentication

### Alliance Considerations
- **Communicate**: Let leadership know you have a farm account
- **Event Participation**: Make sure farm account earns minimum scores for events
- **Don't Exploit**: Share resources fairly; don't hoard farm benefits
- **Respect Policies**: Some alliances have farm account rules - follow them

### Fair Play
> [!TIP]
> Use farm accounts to enhance gameplay, not exploit other players. Many veteran players support farm accounts when used responsibly.

- Don't use farm to hide resources from attackers
- Contribute farm account to alliance wars and events
- Don't overpower with farm account transfers
- Keep farm at reasonable level relative to main

## Farm Account Maintenance Tips

### Daily Farm Account Routine
- **2 minutes**: Check events, claim daily rewards
- **5 minutes**: If event active, participate in farming runs
- **Optional**: Gather resources or train troops

### Weekly Tasks
- Gather resources if needed for main account
- Participate in any major alliance events
- Check building upgrades and queues
- Update main account on caravan findings

### Monthly Review
- Assess if current level is still optimal
- Consider light upgrades if falling behind
- Review event participation trends
- Plan for upcoming events requiring farm help

## Common Farm Account Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake #1: Upgrading Too Much
- Takes forever to manage
- Depletes resources
- Not much extra benefit

### ❌ Mistake #2: Wrong Kingdom/Alliance
- Can't coordinate with main
- Wastes the entire purpose
- Stuck permanently

### ❌ Mistake #3: Ignoring Daily Tasks
- Falls behind in events
- Can't participate meaningfully
- Wastes the account

### ❌ Mistake #4: Not Sharing with Main
- Defeats the purpose of coordinating
- Misses dual-raid opportunities
- Farm becomes useless

### ❌ Mistake #5: Getting Attacked Repeatedly
- Farm gets knocked out during events
- Can't participate during event windows
- Results in wasted time

**Solution**: Keep farm account modest level, move it around, or maintain defensive troops.

## Is a Farm Account Right for You?

### ✅ Create a Farm Account If:
- You have access to a second device or profile
- You're in a competitive alliance doing serious events
- You want to maximize event rewards
- You enjoy multi-tasking and optimization
- Your main account is level 20+

### ⚠️ Consider Alternatives If:
- You only have one device (plays can still work but more tedious)
- You're in a casual alliance
- You prefer simplicity over optimization
- You don't enjoy managing multiple accounts

## FAQ: Farm Accounts

**Q: Will I get banned for having a farm account?**
> A: No, farm accounts are standard in most games. As long as you don't bot or hack, you're fine.

**Q: Can I transfer resources from my farm to main?**
> A: Some events allow resource sharing. Check current alliance policies and game rules.

**Q: How long until a farm is "viable"?**
> A: 2-3 weeks of casual play gets it to event-ready level 15+

**Q: Should I rush my farm to high level?**
> A: No! Levels 20-30 is the optimal farming sweet spot.

**Q: Can my farm account solo carry an event?**
> A: No, but it can be a huge multiplier for your main account.
