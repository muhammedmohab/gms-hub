## 🧠 Grandma’s Secret: Where Damage Comes From

Damage mostly comes from:
- 🟢 Lethality (VERY important)
- ⚔️ Attack

To make your cookies stronger, improve:
- Troop lethality research  
- Deployment capacity  
- Command Center (bigger rallies)  
- Hero gear (Helmet + Boots)  
- Governor charms  
- Pets & refinement  

> [!TIP]
> The more lethality you stack, the sweeter your damage becomes 🍪

---

## ⚔️ Instructions for Rally Leaders (Head Chefs 👩‍🍳)

- Use your **strongest 3 heroes**
- Space rallies ~30 seconds
- Only strong players should lead

### 🧠 How Leading Works:
- Your stats = the WHOLE rally’s power  
- You provide:
  - Research buffs  
  - Gear + charms  
  - Hero stats + skills  
  - Pet + skin + island buffs  

> [!TIP]
> If your hero has a special widget (like Marlin), it buffs everyone in the rally.

---

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Instructions for Rally Joiners (Helpers 🍪)

1. Join rallies instantly  
2. Do NOT send random heroes  
3. Use correct lead heroes (green flag)

> [!TIP]
> Only your FIRST hero matters (the one with the green flag)

![Bear rally joiner heros](https://ibb.co/0RytwL9k)

> [!CAUTION]
> If you don’t have a proper hero → send NO heroes

## 🧩 How Joiners Actually Work

- You give:
  - Troops  
  - ONE skill (from lead hero)

- Only **4 joiner skills** are used in a rally  
- Only **maxed skills (level 5)** count first  

👉 Example:
- Weak player joins first ❌  
- Strong player joins after ✅ → replaces them

### 💥 Buff Stacking:
- Same heroes stack less  
- Different heroes = MORE damage  

> Example:
> - 4 same heroes → 100% boost  
> - Mixed heroes → up to 125% boost 🔥
