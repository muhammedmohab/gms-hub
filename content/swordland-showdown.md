# ⚔️ Swordland Showdown

Welcome commander, today we conquer Swordland in the ultimate showdown! ⚔️🏰

## Objective Focus

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Prioritize the Swordshrine (highest points), Sanctums (high points), and Hall of Reformation (attack buffs).

- **Swordshrine**: Your primary target - maximum points per capture
- **Sanctums**: High-value secondary objectives
- **Hall of Reformation**: Essential for attack buffs to boost your damage

## Teamwork Strategy

> [!TIP]
> Pair a couple top power people together to hold sanctums and Hall of Reformation.

- Strong players should coordinate to secure and maintain control
- Create defensive formations around key objectives
- Communicate with your alliance for coordinated attacks

## Individual Role

> [!NOTE]
> Participate actively to gain individual points, even if you are not the strongest player.

- Every contribution counts toward your personal score
- Focus on objectives you can realistically capture
- Low level players: Search for gold pockets to maximize your earnings

> [!CAUTION]
> Don't overextend - know your limits and focus on achievable targets.

## Healing Management

> [!TIP]
> Utilize the free healing speedups provided for the event to keep your troops in the fight without burning all your resources.

- Save your premium speedups for critical moments
- Use event-provided healing to maintain troop strength
- Time your marches to align with healing cooldowns

## Victory Tips

> [!WARNING]
> Stay aware of your surroundings - enemy counterattacks can happen quickly.

> [!NOTE]
> Balance individual scoring with alliance objectives for maximum benefit.

> Good luck in the Swordland Showdown, brave commander! May your sword strike true and your alliance stand victorious! 🏆⚔️
