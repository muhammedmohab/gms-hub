# 🏪 Merchant Empire

Welcome commander, today we build our merchant empire through caravan mastery! 🏪💰

## Event Basics

> [!IMPORTANT]
> For this event we will be escorting caravans to earn points based on the cart's rarity. This event lasts for 7 days but you can only escort the caravans for 6 days.

- **Duration**: 7 days total, 6 days for escorting
- **Objective**: Escort caravans, earn points based on rarity
- **Higher Rarity = Better Loot**: Focus on the most valuable caravans

## Caravan Management

> [!TIP]
> You can refresh caravans and after 6 refreshes, you are guaranteed to get a Mythic.

- **Refresh Cost**: Gems or caravan vouchers
- **Guaranteed Mythic**: After 6 refreshes
- **Strategy**: If your guaranteed Mythic loot isn't great, refresh again for another

### Escort Limits
- **Maximum Active**: 2 caravans at one time
- **Daily Maximum**: 4 successful escorts per day

## Combat Mechanics

> [!WARNING]
> Other players can attack your caravans and you can attack theirs. If yours is attacked, you will lose loot and points. If attacked twice, you'll lose about 50% in total.

### Attack Indicators
- **Sword Icon**: Player has previously attacked your caravan
- **Fire Icons**: How many times caravan has been "burned" (less loot)

### Raid Limits
- **Daily Maximum**: 4 successful raids per day
- **Same Caravan**: Can attack the same caravan twice

## Protection & Assistance

> [!TIP]
> You can protect caravans by using the "Assisting Squads". This will let you choose someone to help you. Be careful as you can only use this once a day.

- **Assisting Squads**: Choose one helper per day
- **Strategic Use**: Prioritize for higher tier caravans

> [!NOTE]
> You can also offer assistance & get a maximum of 3 rewards based on how well that caravan performs.

## Alliance Brawl Integration

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you see an Alliance Brawl tag on a caravan, attack it to reduce the alliance's points, which then benefits our alliance during Alliance Brawl Event.

- **Alliance Brawl Caravans**: Attack to reduce enemy alliance points
- **Kingdom Hiding**: You can hide our kingdom caravans from enemies

## Strategic Tips

> [!TIP]
> Since most people are active right after reset, avoid launching caravans during this time. But this doesn't mean do it right before reset either. Other players may save their raid attempts to "surprise attack" last minute.

- **Timing**: Avoid peak activity periods
- **Caravan Duration**: Approximately 3.6 hours to complete
- **Rarity Priority**: Focus on higher rarity for better rewards

> [!NOTE]
> Prioritize using assists for higher tier caravans. Depending on what you find harder to get (such as artisan visions, which is very scarce) use that to choose what caravans to ship and raid.

- **Blue Caravans**: Can also have artisan visions
- **Resource Focus**: Target based on your scarcest resources

> [!CAUTION]
> If you've finished your raids for the day, you can share to alliance. PRO TIP: if you have a farm account and located a caravan with good loot, you can share to yourself to raid it.

## Alliance Brawl Coordination

> [!TIP]
> When this event coincides with Alliance Brawl, you can find more of the competing alliance caravans by sharing the location through the event tab and following the caravan to its location.

- **Event Tab Sharing**: Share locations to coordinate attacks
- **Caravan Tracking**: Follow shared caravans to their destinations

> Good luck building your merchant empire, shrewd commander! May your caravans arrive safely and your raids be profitable! 🏪💎
