## Building Priority Order

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Focus on these buildings in this order for optimal island development.

1. **Fountain of Life** - Your water essence source
2. **Reservoir 1 and 2** - Additional water storage capacity

## Resource Considerations

> [!CAUTION]
> The other buildings need resources other than water essence to upgrade. Plan your resource gathering accordingly!

- Water essence is primarily for the priority buildings listed above
- Diversify your resource collection for other building upgrades
- Balance island development with your overall resource strategy

## Development Tips

> [!TIP]
> Start with the Fountain of Life and Reservoirs to establish your water essence foundation, then work through the growth and gathering buildings systematically.

> [!NOTE]
> Troop strength buildings should align with your army's weaknesses. Don't over-invest in one type at the expense of others.

> Good luck developing your island, commander! May your buildings rise tall and your troops grow strong! 🏝️⚔️
