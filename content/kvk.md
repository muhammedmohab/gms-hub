# 🏰 KvK / SvS Guide

Welcome commander, this is your PvP feast for KvK (also called SvS). This event appears around day 70–80 after the server opens, and it is the true endgame test of kingdom strength. We are not fighting our own server — we are fighting another kingdom.

---

## 🔥 What is KvK?
KvK is a 3-phase event that returns every 4 weeks once the server is eligible. Matchmaking begins on Saturday, and then we fight across kingdoms for glory, rewards, and the right to control the enemy King’s Castle.

> [!TIP]
> If you want to dominate KvK, focus on kingdom power, event participation, and coordination.

---

## ⚔️ The Phases of KvK

### 1. MATCHMAKING (2 DAYS)
- Our kingdom is matched with another kingdom of similar power.
- Matchmaking uses top 100 player power and kingdom generation.
- Removing gear or hiding troops in infirmary does NOT affect matchmaking.
- Matchmaking reads permanent stats like governor gear, charms, buildings, research, and other active bonuses.

> [!CAUTION]
> You cannot trick match by hiding soldiers or removing gear.

#### If no match is found
We get Matchmaking Bye Rewards:
- 2,000 Diamonds
- 10 General Mythic Hero Shards
- 100 Kingdom Coins
- 10 × 1hr speed-ups

---

### 2. PREPARATION PHASE (5 DAYS)
This phase is like HoG: complete tasks to earn points. The kingdom with more points wins the phase.

Why win Prep Phase?
- Winner gets the right to attack the losing kingdom’s King’s Castle in Battle Phase.
- Winner’s own castle is safe and cannot be attacked.

#### Rewards during Prep Phase
- Personal Reward Chests unlock daily from points.
- Last chest at 200,000 points gives a State Medal.
- You can claim 1 State Medal per day.
- By day 5, you can earn 5 Medals.
- The 6th medal comes from taking part in the King’s Castle Battle during Battle Phase.
- There are also:
  - Phase Ranking Rewards
  - Alliance Phase Ranking Rewards
  - Total Ranking Rewards

---

### 3. BATTLE PHASE (12 HOURS)
This is when fighting begins.
- Battle Phase runs from 10:00 UTC to 22:00 UTC.
- Cross-Kingdom Teleporters unlock at 10:00 UTC.
- You earn points by attacking enemy Town Centers only when their TC is within 3 levels of your TC.
- Alliance buildings cannot be attacked.

> [!TIP]
> Teleport early, harass the enemy kingdom, and control their resource map before Castle Battle begins.

#### CASTLE BATTLE
- Starts at 12:00 UTC.
- Both kingdoms’ castle positions are removed.
- No titles, no King.
- If the attacking kingdom wins, they appoint a HIGH KING over both kingdoms.
- If the defending kingdom wins, both kingdoms return to normal with regular Kings.
- The alliance that holds the Castle longest appoints the King for that kingdom.

#### Turrets
If your kingdom holds a turret instead of attacking the enemy Castle, the turret grants squad lethality buffs:
- 1 turret → +8% Squad Lethality
- 2 turrets → +12% Squad Lethality
- 3 turrets → +15% Squad Lethality
- 4 turrets → +20% Squad Lethality

> [!TIP]
> Turrets are critical. Hold at least 2 before pushing the Castle.

#### Battle points
You earn points from:
- Occupying turrets or the Castle (1 point every 60s)
- Killing enemy troops (higher level = more points)
- Losing troops (higher level = more points)
- Level 10 troops: 13 points per kill or loss

#### Rewards
- Winner Rewards: all players in the winning kingdom
- Runner-Up Rewards: all players in the losing kingdom (much smaller)
- Personal Ranking Rewards
- Alliance Ranking Rewards

---

## 🩹 FIELD TRIAGE PHASE
This phase recovers troops lost during Castle Battle.
- Base rescue rate is 30%.
- Medical Satchels add +10% rescue each.
- You can rescue up to 90% with Satchels and Rescue Orders.
- Rescue Orders are gifted by alliance members.
- Each member can receive up to 50 Rescue Orders.
- Every member gets 20 Rescue Orders to give.
- Troops that died and did not fit in the infirmary can be rescued here.
- Troops in the Enlistment Office are recovered separately with Loyalty Points.

> [!CAUTION]
> Field Triage only rescues dead troops, not wounded ones in the infirmary.

## 💡 Tips and Tricks
- If our kingdom is being invaded, bubble/shield before Castle Battle starts.
- If we win Prep Phase, teleport to the enemy kingdom and smash their soldiers and resources before Castle Battle.
- Heal in batches. Full healing is slow. Around 30 minutes is usually a sweet spot.
- Avoid having excess troops in infirmary or unnecessary troops taking up space.
- Turrets provide lethality buffs and make Castle capture much easier.
- Garrison turrets carefully: swap garrison troops as needed.
- Lightly injured troops do NOT fight and do NOT go into the infirmary.
- When taking the Castle, use 2–3 big rallies with 2–5 second intervals.
- Read the Bear Maximizing guide for rally mechanics. Rally skills matter for both leaders and joiners.

> [!TIP]
> Understanding rallies is as important as having strong troops.

---

## 🎯 Final Strategy
KvK is about coordination more than raw power.
- Win Prep Phase to secure the attacking advantage.
- Control turrets to stack lethality.
- Use teleports and map pressure wisely.
- Rally smart, heal smart, and protect your kingdom when needed.

> Good luck on KingShot KvK / SvS. Stay sharp and play as one kingdom.
