# 🏆 Eternity's Reach

Welcome commander, today we hunt the richest veins and keep our march ready for every moment. Eternity's Reach is all about timing, XP focus, and smart mining rotations.

## Priority Targets

> [!TIP]
> When you first enter, prioritize attacking Cesares for XP. Keep attacking them whenever you get a chance — their value grows with the skills you choose.

## Mining Rotation

> [!IMPORTANT]
> When you have time, go to a copper vein and begin mining. Aim for level 2 or 3 if you can, but level 1 will still work.

- Start mining immediately once you have a safe vein
- Cycle at least one mining march to a new vein every 60 seconds
- Each cycle gives you a fresh 5000 copper bonus

> [!CAUTION]
> If you are surrounded by higher-level players, teleport to an emptier space. You get free TPs on a cooldown, so use them to avoid losing progress.

## Fracture Veins and Center Control

> [!WARNING]
> When Fracture Veins appear, make them your priority. They provide much more copper and are vital to scoring well.

- Leave one march cycling normal veins while the rest focus on Fracture Veins
- Use that last gathering skill during Fracture Veins for maximum efficiency

> [!TIP]
> Only aim for the center if you can reach it exactly when it opens. Brief control is fine, but if you can't secure it quickly, ignore it and let the whales fight over it.

## Final Advice

A lot of this comes down to luck with who you're paired with, so don't be discouraged if it doesn't work every single time. Keep your focus on XP, mining speed, and smart movement — that is how you win more consistently.

> Good luck, commander — may your copper pile grow and your march stay safe! 💎
