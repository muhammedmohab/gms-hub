# 🛍️ Grandma’s Shop Cookbook  
Welcome back, dear 🍪 Today we’re going shopping—Grandma style 🧺  
Not everything shiny is worth your cookies, so let’s learn how to spend wisely 💛  

---

## 🧭 Where to Find the Shop  
- Look at the **bottom tab**  
- It’s right next to your **Backpack** 🎒  
- Inside, you’ll find **9 different shops**  

> [!TIP]  
> Shops refresh at different times ⏱️  
> Plan ahead so you don’t miss the tastiest deals 🍯  

---

## 🐪 Nomadic Merchant (Daily Refresh 🌅)  
Grandma says: *“Always check the traveling seller!”*  

- Trade for **discounted resources** 🟢  
- Grab **VIP EXP** (if discounted) ⭐  
- Pick up **Advanced Teleports** when needed  

> [!TIP]  
> Discounts here are sweet like honey—don’t ignore them 🍯  

---

## 🎲 Mystery Shop (Daily Refresh 🌅)  
A little gamble never hurt… if you’re smart 😏  

- **Refresh every time** 🔄  
- Look for **FREE items** 🎁  
- Hunt **50% off Widget Chests**  

> [!TIP]  
> Patience + refreshes = hidden treasures 💎  

---

## 🏟️ Arena Shop  
This is NOT a snack shop—this is an investment 🧠  

- 🎯 Save for **Custom Mythic Hero Gear Chest**  
- Got leftovers? Spend carefully  

> [!CAUTION]  
> Gear your **top 3 heroes first** before wasting coins ❌

## 🧪 Trial Shop  
Grandma’s advanced pantry—choices matter a LOT 🧠  

As your kingdom ages, more items unlock. Here’s the priority list:  

### 🥇 Priority Ingredients  

- 🟥 **Mithril**  
  → Only for big spenders going for **red gear**  
  → Useful AFTER gear level 100 + Master Forge 10  

- 🟣 **Charm Design**  
  → F2P & low spenders: this is your bottleneck 🚧  

- 🎁 **Lucky Hero Gear Chests**  
  → Great for upgrading gear (fodder material)  

- 🟡 **True Gold Dust** *(Unlocked after 300 days)*  
  → Needed for **late-game research & buildings** 🏗️  

- 🏛️ **Mythic Decoration Components**  
  → Permanent stat boosts = always tasty 🍪  

---

### 🧩 Everything Else  
- Buy ONLY after priorities are handled  

> [!CAUTION]  
> Random spending = burnt cookies 🔥

- Watch **refresh timers** ⏱️
