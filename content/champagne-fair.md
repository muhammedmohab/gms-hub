## 🍷 What Is Champagne Fair?

Champagne Fair lets you trade extra items and duplicate hero shards for **Fair Vouchers**, then exchange them for powerful upgrade materials.

If you prepare correctly, this event becomes one of the BEST sources of long-term value in Kingshot.
