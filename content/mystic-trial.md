## Optimum Troop Ratios (Infantry/Cavalry/Archer)

Each Mystic Trial location has different terrain that favors certain troop types. Use these ratios for maximum success!

### Coliseum - 50/10/40
- **Best for**: Balanced combat with archer advantage
- **Strategy**: Use archers for ranged attacks, infantry for defense

### Forest of Life - 50/15/35
- **Best for**: Infantry heavy with some cavalry mobility
- **Strategy**: Infantry provides strong defense in wooded areas

### Crystal Cave - 60/20/20
- **Best for**: Infantry dominance in confined spaces
- **Strategy**: Heavy infantry excels in narrow cave passages

### Knowledge Nexus - 50/20/30
- **Best for**: Balanced with cavalry advantage
- **Strategy**: Cavalry for quick strikes, archers for precision

### Molten Fort - 60/15/25
- **Best for**: Infantry heavy with archer support
- **Strategy**: Infantry withstands heat, archers for distance

### Radiant Spire - 50/15/35
- **Best for**: Balanced with archer focus
- **Strategy**: Archers excel at height advantage
