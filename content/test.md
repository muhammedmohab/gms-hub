# 🧪 Test Recipe with Image

Welcome to this test recipe! Here's a delicious cookie recipe with a beautiful image.

## Ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla
- Chocolate chips

## Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F
2. Mix dry ingredients
3. Cream butter and sugar
4. Add egg and vanilla
5. Combine wet and dry
6. Add chocolate chips
7. Bake for 10-12 minutes

> [!NOTE]
> Remember to use room temperature butter for best results!

> [!TIP]
> For extra chewy cookies, chill the dough for 30 minutes before baking.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Don't overbake! Cookies should be golden around the edges but still soft in the center.

> [!WARNING]
> Oven temperatures can vary, so check your cookies at 10 minutes.

> [!CAUTION]
> Hot cookies will be very soft - let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before moving.
